Company Overview

Ngoola (Pty) Ltd is a multidisciplinary engineering and technical services company based in South Africa. We specialise in process engineering, project management, industrial lubrication, and water & wastewater treatment management.

With a strong commitment to innovation, sustainability, and operational excellence, we deliver practical and cost-effective solutions that help our clients achieve long-term infrastructure reliability and performance.

Our Vision

To become a leading engineering and infrastructure services provider in Africa.

Our Mission

To deliver practical, cost-effective, and sustainable engineering solutions that improve infrastructure performance and support long-term operational reliability.

Our Core Services

Process Engineering

Design, optimisation, troubleshooting, plant performance assessments, chemical dosing systems, and detailed technical reports.

  • Process audits & debottlenecking
  • Flowsheet development
  • P&ID reviews

Project Management

End-to-end project delivery using GCC, NEC, and FIDIC contracts. Expert handling of risk, cost control, scheduling, commissioning and handover.

  • Contract administration
  • Risk & cost management
  • Commissioning & handover

Water & Wastewater

Operational support, water quality monitoring, asset management, compliance, operator training, and process optimisation for treatment plants.

  • Water quality monitoring
  • Plant operation & maintenance
  • Operator training programs

Industrial Lubrication

Development of lubrication programs, oil analysis, contamination control, and reliability improvement strategies to extend equipment life.

  • Oil analysis & condition monitoring
  • Lubrication program design
  • Reliability enhancement
