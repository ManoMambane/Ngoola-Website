Company Overview

Ngoola (Pty) Ltd is a multidisciplinary engineering and technical services company providing specialised solutions in process engineering, project management, industrial lubrication, and water and wastewater treatment management. The company supports municipalities, utilities, power stations, and industrial facilities across South Africa and beyond.

Vision

To become a leading engineering and infrastructure services provider in Africa within the water, wastewater, industrial reliability, and infrastructure project sectors.

Mission

To deliver practical, cost-effective, and sustainable engineering solutions that improve infrastructure performance and support long-term operational reliability.
